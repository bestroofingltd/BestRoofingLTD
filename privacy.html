Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 08/02/2026

1. Introduction

Best Roofing Ltd ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we use any personal information we collect about you when you use this website.

2. What information do we collect?

We collect information about you when you complete our "Get a Quote" contact form. This includes:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Address details (if provided in the message)

3. How will we use the information about you?

We collect this information solely to:

  • Process your request for a quote.
  • Contact you regarding your roofing enquiry.
  • Schedule site visits or surveys.

We will never share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage

Your data is securely transmitted via email to our team. We retain this information only for as long as is necessary to provide our services to you or as required by law.

You have the right to request a copy of the information that we hold about you. If you would like a copy of some or all of your personal information, please email us at info@bestroofingltd.co.uk.
