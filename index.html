Roof Repairs & Maintenance

WE HAVE THE EXPERTISE TO HELP

A damaged roof can lead to costly issues if left untreated. We provide fast, efficient, and affordable roof repairs to keep your home safe and dry. From a single slipped tile to storm damage, no job is too small.

  • Identifying and fixing leaks immediately
  • Replacing broken or missing tiles/slates
  • Storm damage clearing and repair
  • Emergency waterproofing

Flat Roofing Solutions

MODERN GRP FIBREGLASS & FELT

Traditional felt roofs are prone to cracking, but our modern flat roofing systems are built to last. We are experts in GRP (Fibreglass) roofing, which offers a seamless, waterproof finish perfect for garages, extensions, and balconies.

  • GRP Fibreglass (20+ year lifespan)
  • High-performance torch-on felt
  • Garage and extension roof specialists
  • Bubble and crack repairs

Specialist Leadwork

TRADITIONAL CRAFTSMANSHIP

Lead is one of the most durable materials on your roof, but it requires skilled hands to install correctly. We handle all aspects of lead flashing, valleys, and box gutters to ensure your roof remains watertight in the harshest weather.

  • Lead flashing repair and replacement
  • Chimney aprons and back gutters
  • Lead welding and bossing
  • Traditional lead valley installation

Fascias, Soffits & Guttering

UPGRADE YOUR ROOFLINE

Old, rotting timber fascias can let damp into your home. We replace them with durable, low-maintenance UPVC fascias and soffits that look great and protect your roof structure for decades.

  • Full removal of rotten timber
  • High-quality UPVC installation
  • Guttering repairs and cleaning
  • Available in White, Black, or Grey

Chimney Care & Repair

SAFE & SECURE CHIMNEYS

Chimneys take the brunt of the wind and rain. If your chimney stack is unstable or the mortar is crumbling, it poses a safety risk. We offer full repointing, capping, and removal services.

  • Chimney repointing (brickwork)
  • Chimney pot installation and capping
  • Lead flashing around the base
  • Complete chimney removal
